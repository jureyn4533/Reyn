Muzan Kibutsuji is a fictional character and the main antagonist in the manga and anime series "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba". He is the first demon ever created, and he possesses immense strength and regenerative abilities. Muzan is portrayed as a cruel and manipulative character who is responsible for turning many humans into demons and causing the deaths of countless people. He is also depicted as having a deep fear of death and is constantly searching for ways to extend his own life. The series follows the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer in order to avenge his family who were killed by Muzan, and his journey to defeat him.
