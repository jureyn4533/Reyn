Inosuke grew up in the mountains, living in isolation and honing his skills as a fighter. He wears a boar's head as a mask, which he believes gives him added strength and protection in battle. Inosuke is a skilled swordsman and is known for his use of a pair of serrated swords.

Throughout the series, Inosuke initially clashes with the main characters, particularly Tanjiro Kamado, but eventually comes to see them as allies and friends. He also undergoes significant character development as he learns to control his impulsiveness and develop a greater sense of teamwork.
