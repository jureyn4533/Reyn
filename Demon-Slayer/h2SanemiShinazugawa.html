Sanemi is the Wind Hashira and Genya's older brother. Even though he's a Hashira, he's a hot-headed individual who lives for the thrill of fighting. He comes off as somewhat hard to please, and it's easy to make him angry. Due to traumatic events concerning demons, Sanemi is incredibly distrusting of them. He refuses the notion that demons and humans could somehow coexist peacefully.

Sanemi is a fierce fighter, and has a rare blood type that even the strongest demons will find alluring. He often uses this as bait to lure opponents in and swiftly kill them. His Wind Breathing techniques are unmatched and, paired with his Demon Slayer Mark, Sanemi is virtually undefeatable. Sanemi may be the most powerful Hashira, but he's not the strongest character in Demon Slayer.
