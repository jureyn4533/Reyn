Zenitsu is initially depicted as a cowardly and anxious character who constantly seeks to escape from danger. However, he also possesses a powerful ability to hear the thoughts of others, which he uses to gain insight into his enemies and allies alike. He also has a unique combat ability, called Thunder Breathing, which allows him to harness the power of lightning in battle.

Throughout the series, Zenitsu undergoes significant character development as he learns to overcome his fear and develop greater courage in the face of danger. He also forms a close bond with the demon slayer, Nezuko Kamado, whom he initially mistakes for a demon but later comes to see as a friend and ally.
