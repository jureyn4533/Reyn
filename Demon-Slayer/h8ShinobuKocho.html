Even amidst the Demon Slayer Corps, Shinobu was a bit of an oddball. She was the former Insect Hashira. Unlike other Demon Slayers, she severely lacks the physical strength to decapitate a demon. However, she developed a fierce, piercing fighting style to compensate.

Instead of physical fighting, Shinobu relies on injectable substances. Often, these substances cause disorientation, and ultimately death. She's quite knowledgeable about medicinal properties and the effects of certain herbals. She uses this to her advantage in a fight and is an excellent healer for the Corps.
