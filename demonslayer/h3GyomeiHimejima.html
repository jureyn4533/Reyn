Gyomei's title suggests he's a stoic, patient man with unwavering willpower. Gyomei is the Stone Hashira and was recruited by Kagaya Ubuyashiki. According to the series' official statistics, Gyomei's physical abilities are unmatched by any of his peers. Standing at a menacing 7'2 and a half, Gyomei is the definition of a gentle giant. He became interested in demon-slaying due to a traumatic event in his childhood.

While living at a temple with other orphans, a demon was unfortunately, which slaughtered most of the children present. However, Gyomei survived, and he was enraged by losing his friends. Although he didn't have any former experience at this point, he charged at the demon and discovered his impressive physical strength. Combined with his Demon Slayer Mark and Breathing Techniques, Gyomei is undoubtedly a strong fighter.
