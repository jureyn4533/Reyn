Nezuko Kamado is a main character in the popular Japanese manga and anime series, "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba." She is the younger sister of the series' protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado. After their family is attacked by a demon and killed, Nezuko is turned into a demon herself, but she retains some of her human emotions and memories, including her love for her brother.

Despite being a demon, Nezuko is shown to be a kind-hearted and compassionate character who is willing to risk her life to protect those she cares about. She has unique abilities as a demon, including the power to shrink her body and enter a sleep-like state to recover from injuries. Throughout the series, Nezuko accompanies her brother on his quest to become a demon slayer and find a cure for her condition, while also using her powers to help him in battles against other demons.
