Muichiro Tokito was the former Mist Hashira. He's been a tremendous force in the Demon Slayer Corps. Using logic to complete his missions, Muichiro is stoic and believes emotions shouldn't be considered in slaying demons. However, he's contradicted such sentiments on multiple occasions.

He can be perceived as rather airheaded since he wanders off on his own and frequently gets carried away by his own thoughts. However, Muichiro is nonetheless an effective demon slayer. Muichiro was able to defeat an Upper Moon all by himself with the help of his Demon Slayer Mark.
