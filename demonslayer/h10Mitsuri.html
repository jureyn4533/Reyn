Mitsuri is the current Love Hashira. She has a unique breathing technique, so she's undoubtedly slain several enemies during her time in the Corps. Despite her title of the Love Hashira, Mitsuri doesn't hesitate when fighting a demon. Instead, she proves herself a deadly combatant. Mitsuri has special combat skills and her skin is approximately eight times denser than an average human.

When Mitsuri activates her Demon Slayer Mark, her power increases exponentially. Mitsuri is a soft-hearted, passionate person who always compliments the people around her. She seems rather preoccupied with her love life and is always on the hunt for a husband. While she hasn't been successful, fans agree her relationship with Obanai Iguro is adorable.
